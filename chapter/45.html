Al-Jathiyah

Allah ke naam se jo Rehman o Raheem hai

1.            Haa Meem

2.            Is kitaab ka nuzul Allah ki taraf se hai jo zabardast aur Hakeem hai.

3.            Haqeeqat yeh hai ke aasmaano aur zameen mein beshumaar nishaniyan hain iman laane walon ke liye

4.            Aur tumhari apni paidaish mein, aur un haiwanaat (jaanwaron) mein jinko Allah (zameen mein) phayla raha hai, badi nishaniyan hain un logon ke liye jo yaqeen laane wale hain.

5.            Aur shab-o-roz (night and day) ke farq o ikhtilaf mein, aur us rizq mein jise Allah aasmaan se naazil farmata hai phir uske zariye se murda zameen ko jila uthata hai, aur hawaon ki gardish mein bahut nishaniyan hain un logon ke liye jo aqal se kaam lete hain.

6.            Yeh Allah ki nishaniyan hain jinhein hum tumhare saamne theek theek bayan kar rahe hain. Ab aakhir Allah aur uski aayaat ke baad aur kaunsi baat hai jispar yeh log iman layenge.

7.            Tabahi hai har us jhoote badh amal shaks ke liye

8.            Jiske saamne Allah ki aayaat padi jaati hain, aur woh unko sunta hai, phir pooray istakbar ke saath apne kufr par is tarah ada rehta hai goya usne unko suna hi nahin. aise shaks ko dardnaak azaab ka musda suna do.

9.            Hamari aayaat mein se koi baat jab uske ilm mein aati hai to woh unka mazaaq bana leta hain. Aise sab logon ke liye zillat ka azaab hai.

10.          Unke aage jahannum hai. Jo kuch bhi unhon ne duniya mein kamaya hai usmein se koi cheez unke kisi kaam na aayegi. Na unke woh sarparast hi unke liye kuch kar sakenge jinhein Allah ko chodh kar unhon ne apna wali bana rakkha hai. Unke liye bada azaab hai.

11.          Yeh Quran sarasar hidayat hai, aur un logon ke liye balaa ka dardnaak azaab hai jinhon ne apne Rubb ki aayaat ko maanne se inkar kiya.

12.          Woh Allah hi to hai jisne tumhare liye samandar ko musakkhar kiya taa-ke uske hukm se kashtiyan usmein chalein aur tum uska fazl talash karo aur shukar-guzar ho.

13.          Usne zameen aur aasmaano ki saari hi cheezon ko tumhare liye musakkhar kardiya, sab kuch apne paas se. is mein badi nishaniyan hain un logon ke liye jo gaur o fikr karne wale hain.

14.          (Aey Nabi) iman laane walon se kehdo ke jo log Allah ki taraf se bure din aane ka koi andesha nahin rakhte, unki harakaton par darguzar se kaam lein taa-ke Allah khud ek giroh ko uski kamayi ka badla de.

15.          Jo koi neik amal karega apne hi liye karega, aur jo burayi karega woh aap hi uska khumyaza (consequence) bhugtega. Phir jaana to sabko apne Rubb hi ki taraf hai.

16.          Isse pehle bani Israel ko humne kitaab aur hukm aur nabuwat ata ki thi, unko humne umda samaan e zeist se nawaza, duniya bhar ke logon par unhein fazilat ata ki.

17.          Aur deen ke maamle mein unhein wazeh hidayat de di.Phir jo ikhtilaf unke darmiyan runama hua woh (nawaqifiyat ki wajah se nahin balke) ilm aa jaane ke baad hua aur is bina par hua ke woh aapas mein ek dusre par zyadati karna chahte thay. Allah qayamat ke roz un maamlaat ka faisla farmadega jinmein woh ikhtilaf karte rahe hain.

18.          Iske baad aey Nabi humne tumko deen ke maamley mein ek saaf shah-raah(shariyat) par qayam kiya hai. Lihaza tum usi par chalo aur un logon ki khwahishaat ka ittiba na karo jo ilm nahin rakhte.

19.          Allah ke muqable mein woh tumhare kuch bhi kaam nahin aa sakte. Zalim log ek dusre ke saathi hain, aur muttaaqiyon ka saathi Allah hai.

20.          Yeh basirat ki roshiniyan hain sab logon ke liye aur hidayat aur rehmat un logon ke liye jo yaqeen layein.

21.          Kya woh log jinhon ne buraiyon ka irtiqaab kiya hai yeh samjhe baithe hain ke hum unhein aur iman laane walon aur neik amal karne walon ko ek jaisa kardenge ke inka jeena aur marna yaksan ho jaye? Bahut bure hukm hain jo yeh log lagate hain.

22.          Allah ne to Aasmaano aur zameen ko barhaqq paida kiya hai aur is liye kiya hai ke har mutannafis ko uski kamayi ka badla diya jaaye. Logon par zulm hargiz na kiya jayega.

23.          Phir kya tumne kabhi us shaks ke haal par bhi gaur kiya jisne apni khwahish e nafs ko apna khuda bana liya aur Allah ne ilm ke bawajood usey gumraahi mein phenk diya aur uske dil aur kaano (ears) par mohar laga di aur uski aankhon par purda daal diya? Allah ke baad ab aur kaun hai jo usey hidayat de? Kya tum log koi sabaq nahin lete?

24.          Yeh log kehte hain ke “zindagi bas yahi hamari duniya ki zindagi hai, yahin hamara marna aur jeena hai aur gardish-e-ayyam (passage of time) ke siwa koi cheez nahin jo humein halaak karti ho.” Dar haqeeqat is maamle mein inke paas koi ilm nahin hai yeh mehaz gumaan ki bina par yeh baatein karte hain.

25.          Aur jab hamari wazeh aayaat unhein sunayi jaati hain to inke paas koi hujjat iske siwa nahin hoti ke utha lao hamare baap dada ko agar tum sacche ho.

26.          Aey Nabi inse kaho Allah hi tumhein zindagi bakashta hai, phir wahi tumhein maut deta hai , phir wahi tumko us qayamat ke din jamaa karega jiske aane mein koi shakk nahin. Magar aksar log jaante nahin hain.

27.          Zameen aur aasmaano ki badshahi Allah hi ki hai, aur jis roz qayamat ki ghadi aa khadi hogi us din baatil parast khasare (nuksan) mein padh jayenge.

28.          Us waqt tum har girooh ko ghuthano (knees) ke bal gira dekhoge. Har girooh ko pukara jayega ke aaye aur apna naame aamal dekhe. Unse kaha jayega: “aaj tum logon ko un aamal ka badla diya jayega jo tum karte rahe thay.

29.          Yeh hamara tayyar karaya hua aamal naama hai jo tumhare upar theek theek shahadat de raha hai, jo kuch bhi tum karte thay usey hum likhwate jaa rahe thay”.

30.          Phir jo log iman laye thay aur neik aamal karte rahe thay unhein unka Rubb apni rehmat mein daakhil karega. Aur yahi sareeh kaamiyabi hai.

31.          Aur jin logon ne kufr kiya tha unse kaha jayega “kya meri aayaat tumko nahin sunayi jaati thin? Magar tumne takabbur kiya aur mujrim bankar rahe.

32.          Aur jab kaha jata tha ke Allah ka waada bar- haqq hai aur qayamat ke aane mein koi shakk nahin, to tum kehte thay ke hum nahin jaante qayamat kya hoti hai, hum to bas ek gumaan sa rakhte hain , yaqeen hum ko nahin hai”

33.          Us waqt unpar unke aamal ki buraiyan khul jayengi aur woh usi cheez ke pher mein aa jayenge jiska woh mazaaq udaya karte thay.

34.          Aur inse kehdiya jayega ke “Aaj hum bhi usi tarah tumhein bhulaye dete hain jis tarah tum is din ki mulaqaat ko bhool gaye thay.Tumhara thikana ab dozakh hai aur koi tumhari madad karne wala nahin hai.

35.          Yeh tumhara anjaam is liye hua hai ke tumne Allah ki aayaat ka mazaaq bana liya tha aur tumhein duniya ki zindagi ne dhoke mein daal diya tha. Lihaza aaj na yeh log dozakh se nikale jayenge aur na inse kaha jayega ke maafi maang kar apne Rubb ko raazi karo”

36.          Pas tareef Allah hi ke liye hai jo zameen aur aasmaano ka maalik aur saare jahan walon ka parwardigar hai.

37.          Zameen aur aasmaano mein badayi usi ke liye hai aur wahi zabardast (most mighty) aur daana (most wise) hai.
