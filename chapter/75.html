Al-Qiyamah

Allah ke naam se Rehman o Raheem hai

1:            Nahin main qasam khata hoon qayamat ke din ki

2:            Aur nahin,main qasam khata hoon malamat karne wale nafs ki

3:            Kya Insan yeh samajh raha hai ke hum uski haddiyon ko jama na kar sakengey?

4:            Kyun nahin?Hum to uski ungliyon ke por por (fingertips) tak theek bana dene par qaadir hai

5:            Magar Insan chahta yeh hai ke aage bhi bad-aamaliyan karta rahe

6:            “Puchta hai” aakhir kab aana hai woh qayamat ka din?

7:            Phir jab deedhe pathra jayenge (sight is dazed)

8:            Aur chaand be-noor ho jayega

9:            Aur chaand Suraj mila kar ek kardiye jayenge,

10:          Us waqt yahi Insan kahega kahan bhaag kar jaun?

11:          Hargiz nahin,wahan koi jaaye panaah na hogi

12:          Us roz tere Rubb hi ke saamne jaakar theharna hoga

13:          Us roz Insan ko uska sab agla pichla kiya karaya bata diya jayega

14:          Balke Insan khud hi apne aap ko khoob jaanta hai

15:          Chahe woh kitni hi maazratein (excuses) kare,

16:          Aey Nabi , Is wahee ko jaldi jaldi yaad karne ke liye apni zubaan ko harakat na do

17:          Isko yaad kara dena aur padhwa dena hamare zimme hai,

18:          Lihaza jab hum isey padh rahe hon us waqt tum iski qiraat ko gaur se sunte raho.

19:          Phir iska matlab samjha dena bhi hamare zimme hai.

20:          Hargiz nahin, asal baat yeh hai ke tum log jaldi haasil hone wali cheez (yani duniya) se muhabbat rakhte ho

21:          Aur aakhirat ko chodh dete ho

22:          Us roz kuch chehre tar-o-taza honge

23:          Apne Rubb ki taraf dekh rahe honge

24:          Aur kuch chehre udas honge

25:          Aur samajh rahe honge ke unke saath qamar toad bartao hone wala hai

26:          Hargiz nahin, jab jaan halaq tak pahunch jayegi

27:          Aur kaha jayega ke hai koi jhaad phook karne wala

28:          Aur aadmi samajh lega ke yeh duniya se judai ka waqt hai

29:          Aur pindli(leg) se pindli(leg) judh jayegi

30:          woh din hoga tere Rubb ki taraf rawangi ka

31:          Magar usne na sach mana , aur na namaz padhi

32:          Balke jhutlaya aur palat gaya

33:          Phir akadta hua apne ghar walon ki taraf chal diya

34:          Yeh rawish tere hi liye sazawaar hai aur tujh hi ko zeib deti hai,

35:          Haan yeh rawish tere hi liye sazawaar hai aur tujh hi ko zeib deti hai,

36:          Kya Insan ne yeh samajh rakkha hai ke woh yunhi mohmal (left alone, unquestioned ) chodh diya jayega?

37:          Kya woh ek hakeer pani ka nutfa (sperm from semen) na tha jo (raham –e-madar mein) tapkaya jata hai?

38:          Phir woh ek lothda(clot) bana, phir Allah ne uska jism banaya aur uske aazaa durust kiye

39:          Phir us se mard aur aurat ki do qismein banayi

40:          Kya woh ispar qaadir nahin hai ke marne walon ko phir zinda karde?
